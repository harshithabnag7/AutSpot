I am Different, Not less!

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects communication, social interaction, and behavior, it affects 1 in 68 children. Individuals with autism may struggle to understand and interpret social cues, leading to difficulty in forming and maintaining relationships. They may also have repetitive behaviors or interests, as well as sensory processing differences. While there is no known cure for autism, ASD costs a family around 2 million $ on an average. Early intervention with therapies can help individuals with ASD learn and develop skills to navigate daily life more effectively.

We help people on Spectrum to overcome episodes.

They for sure are different, but not less.

How do we work?

A platform designed to help people with autism to help people with autism and their families navigate the challenges that come with this condition. We provide information on education, therapy, and other services that can help your loved ones thrive. Our application scans the face, detects emotion and suggests therapy techniques for people having episodes. We also offer support groups and forums where you can connect with other families who are facing similar challenges.
